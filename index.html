WHEEL REFINISHING

Our wheel refinishing service in Los Angeles is fast, reliable and easy. Wheels are taken off the car, cleaned, repaired, primed, painted, and clear-coated using patented paint adhesion technology.

Wheel repair is usually done on wheels with surface scratches, corrosion, or peeling clear coats on the wheel face, lip, and spokes.

Prior to repair, each wheel is color matched according to OEM requirements.

REMANUFACTURING

Our remanufacturing services are the preferred solution for LA cars with extensive damage on their wheels that are unable to be fixed. This service includes welding, CNC machining, powder coating, and vibratory polishing.

WHEELS

When a wheel is extensively damaged and cannot be fixed, we recommend wheel replacement in Los Angeles. Our large inventory of replacement wheels makes us the perfect partner, offering OEM wheels for almost any kind of vehicle.

WHEEL
STRAIGHTENING

Wheel straightening is a process that is used to repair bent or damaged wheels, out-of-round in the front or back barrel. The wheel is placed in a vise-like device and then pressure is applied to the wheel until it is straight. This can be used to repair both aluminum and steel wheels.
